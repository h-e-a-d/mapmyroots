About MapMyRoots

Making family history preservation accessible, free, and privacy-focused for everyone.

Our Mission

To empower families worldwide to create, visualize, and preserve their family histories without barriers. We believe that documenting your heritage should be free, simple, and private - accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford expensive genealogy software.

Our Story

MapMyRoots was born from a simple frustration: why is creating a family tree so expensive and complicated? Most genealogy services charge monthly fees, lock your data behind paywalls, and make the process overwhelming for beginners.

We created MapMyRoots to solve this problem. Launched in 2023, our platform provides a completely free, intuitive family tree builder that respects your privacy. Your data stays on your device - we never see it, we never store it, and we never sell it.

Since our launch, over 15,000 families have used MapMyRoots to preserve their heritage. From students completing school projects to grandparents documenting multiple generations, our tool serves families worldwide in their journey to understand where they come from.

Why Is MapMyRoots Free?

Great question! We believe family history preservation is a fundamental human activity that shouldn't be monetized. Unlike other genealogy services, we don't charge because:

  • Privacy-focused architecture: Since your data stays on your device, we don't need expensive servers or databases
  • No historical records: We focus on tree visualization, not genealogical research databases
  • Sustainable model: Our lean operation and efficient technology keep costs minimal
  • Community-driven: We're committed to serving families, not shareholders

We will never introduce paid features, subscriptions, or premium tiers. MapMyRoots will remain 100% free, forever.

Our Technology

MapMyRoots is built with modern web technologies to ensure a fast, secure, and reliable experience:

  • HTML5 Canvas: Smooth, responsive family tree visualization
  • LocalStorage API: Secure, local data storage on your device
  • Responsive Design: Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • Advanced Export: High-resolution PNG, PDF, and JSON formats
  • Internationalization: Multi-language support for global accessibility
